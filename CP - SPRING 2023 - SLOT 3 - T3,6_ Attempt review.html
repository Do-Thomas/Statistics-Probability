    Flag question

    Question text

    You roll a  fair die until it shows a six. What is the expected number of times of rolling?


    a.

    None of these

    b.

    3

    c.

    12

    d.

    6

    e.

    4

    Feedback

    Let X be the number of times of rolling until a six comes up. Then X~Geometric(p=1/6).

    E(X) = 1/p = 6.
